Chicken Tenders

The chicken tenders are deep fried boneless, skinless chicken breasts cut into pieces about half a inch thick

Ingridients

  • boneless chicken breast
  • eggs
  • italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • vegetable oil
  • garlic powder
  • all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Whisk garlic powder into eggs, season breadcrumbs generously with salt and pepper
  2. Coat chicken strips in flour, then dip in egg, then breadcrumbs back in egg, then back in breadcrumbs
  3. heat 2" oil to 325 degrees
  4. Fry chicken, in batches, turning once, until golden and an instant-read thermometer inserted in thickest part of each piece registers 160 degrees, about 5 minutes
  5. Transfer chicken to prepared rack and let sit for 5 minutes before serving
