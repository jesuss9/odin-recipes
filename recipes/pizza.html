Pizza

Pizza is flattened bread dough spread with a savory mixture usually including tomatoes and chese and often other toppings and baked

Ingridients

  • fresh cilantro
  • sliced mushrooms
  • big handful of pepperoni
  • bag of mozzarella cheese
  • fresh pizza dough lump
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients, heat the sauce while chopping up the ingredients
  2. Prepare the dough
  3. Add the toppings, layer cheese then pepperoni last, all other ingredients underneath
  4. Heat and then enjoy
